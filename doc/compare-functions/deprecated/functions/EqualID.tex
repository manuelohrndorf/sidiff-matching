\comparefunction{EqualID}

\precondition
Requires that the ID attribute exists.

\semantics
Compares two nodes based on their ID.

\returnvalue
In case the IDs are equal the similarity is 1,
otherwise it is 0. 

\parameters
-

\exceptions
\fxnote{Was passiert, wenn ID-Attribut nicht existiert?}

%
